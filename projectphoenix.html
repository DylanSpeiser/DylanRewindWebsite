Project Phoenix: a Dylan Rewind Story

premieres May 13, 2025 at the UMD STAMP Student Union

Doors open 6:30 PM for 7:00 PM EDT Showtime

"Project Phoenix", a secretive governmental organization, has descended upon College Park. As more and more of his friends get captured, Dylan struggles to find his place in the ever-changing social landscape around him. His search for meaning extends both inwards and outwards as the restless Agents of Project Phoenix track him down. There's something in him that only the Agents can see, and they aren't willing to wait any longer to learn what makes Dylan 'tick'.

Project Phoenix: a Dylan Rewind Story stands as the incredible achievement of the more than 40 members of its cast and crew, and their consistent effort for more than a year and a half. Dylan's journey is somewhat universal, even if you've never once wondered if you, too, were being tracked by secret agents. Maybe you've felt lonely, or like the person you've become is too different than the one that started your journey. But that's exactly the question we're trying to answer, so Welcome to the Project...
