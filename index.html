The father of Dylan Rewind, Dylan has been making movies for as long as he's been alive. Honing his skills since the day he grasped a camera, Dylan excels at cinematography and editing. Of course, Dylan Rewind could not be possible without the unwavering support of Dylan's friends and family, who, for some reason, participate in these shenanigans.
