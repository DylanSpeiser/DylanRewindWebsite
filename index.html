Dylan Speiser

The father of Dylan Rewind, Dylan has been making movies for as long as he's been alive. Honing his skills since the day he grasped a camera, Dylan excels at cinematography and editing. Dylan also enjoys acting in front of the camera, as well as onstage. He pours his soul into storytelling, especially through technology. Dylan Rewind is but one of these outlets. Of course, Dylan Rewind could not be possible without the unwavering support of Dylan's friends and family, who, for some reason, participate in these shenanigans.
